Case Studies

Department of Education, Skills and Employment Redesign

A detailed assessment of how I approached a solo ground-up, responsive web and mobile redesign using iterative user research and ideation to produce a more engaging solution for a website required to provide complex information for a wide range of specific groups with different needs ranging from parents with young children to business owners.



Edgar's Mission Case Study

As part of a group and in consultation with Edgar’s Mission we researched and designed a responsive solution for their main interaction platform (desktop) with scope for future work in mobile responsive design to aid the growth of this not-for-profit organisation. This was a rewarding and educational project. Please click the image to see my contribution.




AR Studio Case Study

As a group of four we were tasked to come up with a new way to engage shoppers online, AR Studio was the result after user testing and interviews in relation to current perception of Augmented Reality technology and its usefulness as a retail engagement tool. Our research exposed many shortfalls in the current use of AR so we researched how we can make this experience more tangible and useful for online shoppers.
