My Background

With a diverse background in Sales within the finance and motorsport industries I have strong interpersonal skills, experience with presenting complex propositions to clients and stakeholders and posses keen problem solving skills.

After achieving success in these industries I've sought to develop my passion for technology, software and design through further study in UX/UI at Monash University.

Experience and Education


Education


Monash University

Monash University UX/UI Boot Camp: Whilst working full time, six days a week at Revolution Racegear using the skills I developed with RMIT I discovered that problem solving and design are skills that I’m not just passionate about but am also driven to achieve greater things with. Upon starting this course, despite the fast pace it was immediately apparent that User Experience, User Interface design and Coding is something I have an affinity for and is closely aligned with my prior education and career experience which has revolved around customer focused solutions and problem solving. I am seeking an opportunity to put these skills to use in the industry to design user focused solutions which help to bridge the gap from users to businesses.

Monash University

Bachelor of Arts: My tertiary education started at Monash University in Melbourne, Australia with a Bachelor of Arts degree completed with a double major in Philosophy and Sociology. Within this degree I covered a wide range of minor units including Psychology but found a particular interest in learning about complex concepts, ethics and the understanding of people. The sociological component focused on understanding groups of people, what drives them and motivates them to take action. I’ve found this background useful in the completion of case studies and the identification of problems during the user research phase providing valuable insights into the approaches taken to design a solution.


Experience


Revolution Racegear

After making the decision to leave the finance industry I sought to move into an industry I am passionate about with the capacity to build my underlying yet unharnessed design talents via self-driven learning. Working in a Sales and Marketing position for three years I had the pleasure of meeting and working with some of the industry’s greatest talents and on a daily basis I had the opportunity to work with a diverse range of customers to understand not just what they wanted but what they may also need to achieve their personal goals in motorsport and ensure their safety. Over this time I managed the national social media platforms and worked hard to develop contemporary, engaging and professional standard content which included end-to-end planning, product photography, designing digital assets, refining a style guide, social media strategy, briefing franchises and distributing assets for consistent use online and executing digital campaigns for new products.
